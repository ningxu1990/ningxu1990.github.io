I am currently pursuing my Ph.D., supervised by Professor An-An Liu at Tianjin University in China. I mainly focus on the intersection of computer vision and machine learning. My recent research interest is to develop deep learning techniques for vision captioning tasks. I am a member of the Multimedia Institute. I was a visiting scholar from March, 2016 to March, 2017 in the SeSaMe Research Centre of the National University of Singapore, working with Yongkang Wong. I received my M.E. degree from Tianjin University in 2015 and B.E. degree from Hainan University (Advanced Class) in 2013.
