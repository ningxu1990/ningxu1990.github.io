Research Overview

My research is centered on Machine Learning, with a primary focus on Deep Learning techniques. I engage in a variety of applications such as Image/Video Captioning, Text-to-Image Generation, Visual Question Answer, Visual Dialog, Scene Graph Generation, Action Recognition, and Causality.
